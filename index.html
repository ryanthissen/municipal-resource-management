Municipal Resource Management allows citizens, city planners, and engineers to view locational and graphical informaiton about water and energy usage at city maintained facilities. Using this, decsisions can be made about which locations need to increase conservation measures, or which facilities to consider replacing old appliances and infrastructure with more efficient models.
